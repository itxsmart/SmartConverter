Frequently Asked Questions

Are your PDF tools free to use?

Yes! All our PDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden charges. We don't add watermarks to your processed files either.

Is my data secure when using your tools?

Absolutely! All file processing happens directly in your browser. We don't upload your files to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file size limits do you have?

Most of our tools can handle files up to 50MB. For larger files, the processing might take longer depending on your device's capabilities.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No account registration is required! You can use all our PDF tools immediately without signing up. However, creating an account gives you access to additional features.

Which browsers are supported?

Our tools work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version for the best experience.

How can I report issues or suggest improvements?

You can use the contact form above to report bugs or suggest new features. We actively listen to user feedback and continuously improve our tools.
