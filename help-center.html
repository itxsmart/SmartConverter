Getting Started

How do I start using PDF Tools?
Simply visit our homepage and choose from any of our available PDF tools. No registration is required for basic usage. Just upload your PDF file and select the tool you want to use.
Are there any file size limits?
For optimal performance, we recommend files under 50MB. Larger files may take longer to process or may require registration for premium features.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required for basic PDF operations. However, creating a free account gives you access to additional features, processing history, and premium tools.

PDF Tools

Which file formats are supported?
We support PDF, Word (DOC/DOCX), Excel (XLS/XLSX), PowerPoint (PPT/PPTX), JPG, PNG, and many other common formats. The supported formats vary by tool.
How do I merge multiple PDF files?
Use our PDF Merge tool. Upload multiple PDF files, arrange them in your desired order, and click "Merge PDFs". Your combined file will be ready for download in seconds.
Can I convert password-protected PDFs?
Yes, but you'll need to provide the password during the upload process. We support both user passwords and owner passwords for PDF conversion.

Troubleshooting

My file won't upload. What should I do?
Check your internet connection and ensure your file isn't corrupted. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the problem persists, try using a different browser or contact our support team.
The conversion is taking too long. Why?
Processing time depends on file size, complexity, and current server load. Large files or complex documents may take several minutes. Please be patient and avoid refreshing the page.
My converted file looks different. What happened?
Some formatting may change during conversion due to differences between file formats. This is normal, especially with complex layouts. Try using our advanced conversion options for better results.

Security & Privacy

Is my data safe and secure?
Absolutely! All file processing happens in your browser using client-side technology. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Do you store my files?
No, we do not store any of your files. All processing is done locally in your browser, and files are automatically deleted from your device's temporary memory when you close the browser tab.
Can I use this for confidential documents?
Yes! Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your confidential documents never leave your device. This makes our tools perfect for sensitive business documents.
