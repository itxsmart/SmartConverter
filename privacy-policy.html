Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Quick Summary

We prioritize your privacy: All PDF processing happens in your browser. We don't store, access, or share your documents. No registration required for basic tools. Your files remain completely private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information only when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Account Registration: Email address, username, and password (if you choose to create an account)
  • Contact Forms: Name and email when you contact our support team
  • Newsletter: Email address if you subscribe to updates

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical data to improve our services:

  • IP address and general location (country/city level)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device information and screen resolution
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referrer website information

File Processing Data

Important: Your PDF files and documents are processed entirely in your browser using client-side technology. We never upload, store, or access your files on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our PDF tools and website functionality
  • User Support: To respond to your questions and provide technical assistance
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our tools
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Communications: To send important updates about our services (if you've opted in)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. File Processing & Security

Client-Side Processing

All PDF operations (merge, split, convert, compress, etc.) are performed directly in your web browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Security Measures

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • No File Storage: We don't store any uploaded or processed files
  • Memory Clearing: Browser memory is cleared after processing
  • No Third-Party Access: Your documents are never shared with third parties

4. Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use cookies that are necessary for basic website functionality:

  • Session management and user authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Basic website functionality and preferences

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website:

  • Page views and user interactions
  • Popular tools and features
  • Technical performance metrics
  • Anonymized demographic information

Cookie Control

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Data brokers or marketing companies
  • Social media platforms for advertising
  • Any company for commercial purposes
  • Government agencies (except when legally required)

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share anonymized, aggregated data in these specific cases:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and security services that help operate our website
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Contact Inquiries: Retained for 2 years for support purposes
  • Processed Files: Never stored or retained

7. Your Rights & Choices

Under GDPR (EU Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

Under CCPA (California Users)

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Equal service and price even if you exercise privacy rights

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted and operated from servers located in various countries. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your residence, which may have different data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes (if you have an account)

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
